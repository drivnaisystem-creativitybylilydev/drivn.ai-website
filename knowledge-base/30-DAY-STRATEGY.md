# 30-Day Strategic Focus (May 6 - June 6, 2026)

**Current State:** $165 MRR, 377 sourced leads, comprehensive agent infrastructure built  
**Goal:** Validate home service contractor niche and reach $500+ MRR  
**Core Insight:** You're overbuilt and undervalidated. Deploy what you have, don't build more.

---

## The Real Problem

You have excellent infrastructure but **zero revenue from cold outreach**. The next 30 days must answer one question: **Do home service contractors actually buy what you're selling?**

Stop optimizing. Start selling.

---

## Priority 1: Deploy Tier 1 Demo (Week 1–2)

**Action:** Build one complete Tier 1 system ($800 setup, $100/mo) end-to-end.

**What it includes:**
- Professional website
- Google review automation
- Basic CRM tracking

**Why:** This is your proof-of-concept and sales tool. You need to validate the entire loop (prospect → audit → proposal → payment → delivery) with real money before scaling.

**Success:** 1–2 week build. Use as reference for future clients.

---

## Priority 2: Efficient Outreach, Not Volume (Weeks 1–4)

**Action:** Email your top 20–30 scored leads from the 377 you have. Personalize each. Measure response rate.

**Target metrics:**
- 20% response rate (4–6 responses from 20 emails)
- 3–5 audits booked
- 1–2 proposals sent

**Why:** High-volume, low-conversion tells you your message is broken. You need data to validate the niche. Better to nail messaging with 20 emails than spray 200 emails that don't convert.

**Don't do:** Bulk email all 377. That's wasting your best asset (personalization) and hiding signal in noise.

---

## Priority 3: Sales Data Collection (All 30 days)

Track religiously in a spreadsheet or CRM:

| Metric | Target |
|--------|--------|
| Emails sent | 20–30 |
| Response rate | 15–25% |
| Audits booked | 3–5 |
| Proposals sent | 1–2 |
| Deals closed | 1–2 |
| Setup fees collected | $800–$1,600 |
| Monthly retainers | $100–$200 |

**Why:** This becomes your unit economics. If CAC = $300 and LTV = $2,400/year, you scale aggressively. If not, you pivot.

---

## Priority 4: Avoid Feature Creep

**Don't build in the next 30 days:**
- Lead Nurture Agent
- Proposal Writer Agent
- Case Study Builder
- Tier 2/3 demo systems
- Custom integrations

You have enough infrastructure. Deploy what exists. Polish later.

**The temptation:** Build more, optimize more, add more features. **Resist.** Messy wins beat perfect builds.

---

## Success Criteria for June 6

✅ Tier 1 demo deployed and live  
✅ 20–30 cold emails sent with >15% response rate  
✅ 3–5 audits booked  
✅ 1–2 customers signed ($1,200+ setup fees)  
✅ MRR grows from $165 → $500+  
✅ Repeatable sales playbook validated with data

**If you hit these:** Niche is validated. Scale aggressively.  
**If you miss:** Message/targeting/positioning is wrong. Pivot strategy, not features.

---

**Week 3 (May 20–26):** Audits completed, proposals drafted, close deals

This is a **validation sprint**, not a scaling phase. Everything is temporary until you prove the unit economics work.
