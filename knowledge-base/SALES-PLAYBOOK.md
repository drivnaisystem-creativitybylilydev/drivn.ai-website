# DRIVN.AI — Sales Playbook
*Last updated: 2026-05-06*

## Pain Point Story (Use in ALL outreach)

*"Every missed call is a job that goes to someone else. Every lead that waits is a lead that books with a competitor. It happens every day — and most businesses accept it as the cost of doing business. We don't."*

## Cold Email Template 1: Initial Contact

**Subject:** Quick question about [Business Name]'s call answering

Hi [Owner first name],

I noticed [Business Name] gets great reviews on Google — your team knows how to do the work well.

But I'm curious: when you're out on a job and the phone rings, how often do you miss the call?

It's a quick question, but the answer usually tells us if there's a revenue opportunity hiding in plain sight.

Would be happy to walk through what we're seeing in your niche (takes 15 min max).

— Finn

P.S. — No pitch. Just a conversation to see if there's a fit.

---

## Free Audit Questions (15 minutes)

1. "How many inbound calls do you get per week?"
2. "Of those, how many do you miss?"
3. "What happens to those missed calls?"
4. "If you could capture the missed calls, what would that be worth?"
5. "Would you be open to a system that captures most without adding headcount?"

## Qualification Framework

### Must Have (All)
- 15+ calls/week
- Missing 5+ calls/week
- 4+ star rating
- $1,500+ job value
- Owner busy in field
- Local service area

### Disqualifiers (Any one)
- <10 calls/week
- Already have receptionist
- Bad reviews (<4 stars)
- Price shopping
- National chain
