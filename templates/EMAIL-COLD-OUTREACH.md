# Email Templates — Cold Outreach

## Template 1: Initial Contact

**Subject:** Quick question about [Business Name]'s call answering

Hi [Owner first name],

I noticed [Business Name] gets great reviews on Google — your team obviously knows how to do the work well.

But I'm curious: when you're out on a job and the phone rings, how often do you miss the call?

It's a quick question, but the answer usually tells us if there's a revenue opportunity hiding in plain sight.

Would be happy to walk through what we're seeing in your niche (takes 15 min max).

— Finn

P.S. — No pitch. Just a conversation to see if there's a fit.

---

## Template 2: Follow-Up (3 Days, No Response)

**Subject:** Re: Quick question about [Business Name]'s call answering

Hi [Owner first name],

Just following up in case my last email got buried.

Quick question: do you find yourself missing calls when you or your team is out in the field?

(If yes, we can probably help. If no, no worries — skip this.)

— Finn
