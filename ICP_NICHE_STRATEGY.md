1. **Missed Call Detection**

   - Cal.com or similar (free tier)
   - Link in every SMS
   - Reduces phone tag

5. **Tracking Dashboard**
   - Google Sheet with all data
   - Calls received, missed, responded, booked
   - Real-time visibility

6. **Monthly ROI Report**
   - Calls missed → SMS sent → Responses → Appointments → Jobs closed
   - Revenue recovered calculation
   - Proof of value

**Tech Stack (Same Every Time):**
- Make.com (automation workflow)
- Twilio ($10-20/month for SMS)
- Google Sheets (tracking/CRM)
- Cal.com (appointment booking)

**Delivery Time:**
- Setup: 2-4 hours (one Make.com scenario + Twilio config)

## NICHE TESTING METHODOLOGY (IF HOME SERVICES DOESN'T WORK)

### The Right Way to Test Niches

**DO NOT test multiple niches simultaneously. It doesn't work.**

**Proper Testing = 60 Days, One Niche, Deep Focus**

### The 60-Day Niche Test

**Week 1-2: Research & Setup**
- Scrape 100 leads in target niche
- Enrich top 50 (check Instagram, website, call to test response)
- Build outreach list with notes
- Write 3 message variations to A/B test

**Week 3-4: Outreach Wave 1**
- Send 25 messages (test message variation A)
- Track: Opens, responses, objections, call bookings
- Log every response and objection
- Adjust messaging based on feedback

**Week 5-6: Outreach Wave 2**
- Send 25 more messages (improved message B based on learnings)
- Book 5+ audit calls
- Run calls, deeply understand their pain points
- Take detailed notes on what they care about

**Week 7-8: Close & Analyze**
- Send 3-5 proposals based on audit calls
- Close 1-2 clients minimum
- Document: What worked, what didn't, common objections, close reasons

**End of 60 Days: Decision Point**

**How to use this:**

After 60 days in a niche, fill out the scorecard:
- Response rate: (Responses / Messages Sent) × 100

- Close rate: (Deals Closed / Proposals Sent) × 100
- Avg deal size: Total MRR / Clients Closed
- Referral rate: (Referrals / Total Clients) × 100

**If 3+ metrics hit target = VALIDATED NICHE. Stop testing, scale this.**

**If <3 metrics hit target = PIVOT to next niche, test for 60 days.**

---

### Signs a Niche is GOOD

✅ **They respond to cold outreach** (>15% response rate)
- Your messaging resonates
- Pain point is real and felt
- Timing is right (they need this now)

✅ **They book calls easily** (>20% of responses convert to calls)
- They're curious and open
- They have decision-making authority
- They're actively looking for solutions

✅ **They have budget** ($250-500/month is not a problem)
- Right economic segment
- Understand value of time/revenue
- Will invest to solve real pain

✅ **They see immediate value** (understand ROI without heavy education)
- Pain → Solution connection is obvious
- Numbers make sense to them
- Can visualize the outcome

✅ **They refer others organically** (>10% referral rate)
- Tight-knit community (contractors know contractors)
- They talk about vendors with peers
- Success is visible to others

✅ **Scalable market** (can find 1,000+ of them easily)
- Large enough to build a business
- Accessible via scraping/directories
- Concentrated geographically or online

---

### Signs a Niche is BAD

❌ **Ghost your outreach** (<10% response rate)
- Pain point isn't real or pressing
- Wrong messaging or wrong channel
- They're oversaturated with vendors

❌ **Respond but never book calls** (tire kickers)
- Curious but not serious
- No authority to decide
- Just gathering information

❌ **Book calls but can't afford it** (wrong economic segment)
- Interested but no budget
- Price-sensitive niche
- Value proposition doesn't justify cost

❌ **Don't understand the value** (heavy education required)
- Abstract benefit, hard to quantify
- Long sales cycle
- Need to "build a case" internally

❌ **Islands** (don't talk to each other, no referrals)
- Referrals don't happen naturally
- Each sale is isolated
- No network effect

❌ **Tiny market** (only 50-100 prospects total)
- Hit ceiling fast
- Can't scale beyond initial batch
- Not a sustainable business
